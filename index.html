What is LeggoChat?

General purpose annonymous chat
clear_all

No chat records stored

All the chats are annonymous and no records will be stored unless you choose to persist. Once the participants refresh the pages, it is all gone. None will be logged and no residual storage.

group_add

Easily create a group

Create a group by joining or creating a channel, then send the link to your friends to start chatting annonymously. Channels are not password protected and is open to public if they know the link or channel name.

visibility_off

No names

No names, just encrypted strings to identify different entities. It is that annonymous. Make your channel as unique as possible to ensure that you are indeed chatting with invited parties.
